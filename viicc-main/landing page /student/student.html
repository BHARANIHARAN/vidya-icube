What is Student Innovation Seed Fund?

The Student Innovation Seed Fund is a pioneering initiative designed to empower young innovators by providing financial support and mentorship for transformative ideas. This program offers early-stage funding to student entrepreneurs, helping them convert promising concepts into viable prototypes and ventures. With grants ranging from ₹25,000 to ₹1,00,000, the fund targets projects that demonstrate exceptional creativity, technical feasibility, and potential for real-world impact.

Beyond financial assistance, recipients gain access to expert mentorship, specialized workshops, networking opportunities, and state-of-the-art facilities to accelerate their innovation journey. The fund particularly encourages solutions addressing pressing societal challenges, sustainable development, and emerging technologies. By investing in student-led innovation, the program aims to cultivate the next generation of entrepreneurs and changemakers who will drive meaningful progress in our communities and beyond.

Eligibility & Categories

College Students

Undergraduate and graduate students enrolled in accredited institutions can showcase their innovative ideas and technical skills.

Project Types: Hardware, Software, IoT, AI/ML

Funding Available: Up to ₹50,000 per team

Team Size: 1-4 members

Mentorship: Access to industry experts

Young Professionals

Early career professionals working in technology or related fields with innovative solutions to real-world problems.

Project Types: Industry Solutions, Startups, Research

Funding Available: Up to ₹75,000 per team

Age: Under 30 years

Team Size: 1-4 members

Benefits: Networking with investors

High School Students

Students enrolled in secondary education institutions with a passion for technology and innovation (special category).

Project Types: STEM Projects, Mobile Apps, Robotics

Funding Available: Up to ₹25,000 per team

Age: 14-18 years

Team Size: 1-4 members

Special Benefits: College recommendation letters, Internship opportunities

Student Programs

How can I apply for the Student Innovation Seed Fund?

To apply for the Student Innovation Seed Fund (SISF), follow these steps:

  1. Fill out the online application form
  2. Submit your project proposal
  3. Attach necessary documentation
  4. Wait for the review committee's response

Applications are accepted throughout the academic year.

What types of projects are eligible for funding?

We fund innovative projects in various domains including:

  • Technology and Software Development
  • Renewable Energy Solutions
  • Healthcare Innovations
  • Agricultural Technology
  • Smart City Solutions

Faculty Programs

What is the Faculty Innovation Seed Fund?

The Faculty Innovation Seed Fund (FISF) is a funding program designed to support faculty members in their research and development initiatives. It provides financial assistance for:

  • Research Projects
  • Prototype Development
  • Patent Filing
  • Conference Presentations
How long is the funding period?

The funding period typically lasts for one academic year, with the possibility of extension based on project progress and requirements. Regular progress reports are required to maintain funding.

Facilities & Resources

What facilities are available for project work?

Our center offers various facilities including:

  • Research Laboratories
  • Prototyping Workshop
  • Computing Resources
  • Meeting Rooms
  • Conference Hall

All facilities are available during working hours and can be booked in advance.

How can I access the research facilities?

To access research facilities:

  1. Submit a facility access request form
  2. Get approval from your department head
  3. Complete the safety training (if required)
  4. Book the facility through our online portal
